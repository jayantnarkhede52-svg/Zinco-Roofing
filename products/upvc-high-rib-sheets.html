The Acid-Test for Industrial Roofing

When it comes to chemical manufacturing plants in the Taloja MIDC or coastal warehouses in Uran, traditional metal sheets often fail within a few years due to rapid oxidation. Zinco's UPVC High-Rib Sheets in Navi Mumbai are engineered specifically to solve this problem.

Our 3-layer co-extrusion technology features a high-density UPVC core enveloped by weather-resistant ASA layers. This structure is totally impervious to acid fumes, alkaline solutions, and high-salinity coastal air, ensuring a lifespan of over 25 years in environments where galvanized steel would deteriorate in five.

Thermal Comfort

Low thermal conductivity ensures that indoor warehouse temperatures remain up to 8°C cooler during peak Navi Mumbai summers.

Rain Noise Suppression

The multi-layer composite structure absorbs impact energy, making it significantly quieter than metal during heavy monsoons.

Electrical Insulator

Non-conductive properties provide an extra layer of safety for high-voltage industrial facilities.

Engineered for High-Performance Warehouses

The high-rib profile of our UPVC High-Rib Sheets in Navi Mumbai isn't just for show. The 37mm high ribs provide exceptional load-bearing capacity and water drainage, preventing the "ponding" effect that leads to leaks in flatter roof profiles.

At Zinco, we provide full technical support for purlin spacing and fastening methods to ensure your UPVC roof remains structurally sound even under extreme wind loads and torrential rainfall.

Industrial UPVC FAQ

Will the color fade in the Navi Mumbai sun?

No, the ASA top layer is specifically designed for high-UV environments and maintains 95% of its color vibrancy even after a decade of exposure.

How does it compare to Galvalume in cost?

While the initial cost of UPVC High-Rib Sheets in Navi Mumbai is slightly higher than basic metal sheets, the elimination of painting and the 3x longer lifespan makes it the cheapest choice per year of service.
