Leading Corrugated Roofing Solutions

Zinco's signature color coated roofing sheets are engineered on a high-tensile Cold Rolled (CR) steel base with a protective hot-dip metallic coating. This multi-stage process ensures unmatched resistance to the corrosive saline atmosphere of the Mumbai coast.

Our products utilize Cold Rolled Full Hard (CRFH) steel for superior structural integrity, allowing them to withstand significant wind loads. Each sheet is chemically pre-treated and finished with a high-durability polyester primer and a lead-free top-coat for a 25-year lifecycle.

Unmatched Durability & Protection

When investing in color coated roofing sheets, durability is paramount. Our sheets feature a robust metallurgical bond between the steel and the zinc coating, acting as a sacrificial barrier that prevents rust even if the surface is scratched during installation.

The UV-resistant paint layer ensures that the vibrant colors do not fade under the intense sun. This thermal performance keeps internal factory temperatures stable, making them a cost-effective solution for high-heat industrial zones.

Maximized Structural Spans

The high-rib trapezoidal profile of our color coated roofing sheets allows for wider purlin spacing, reducing overall structural steel consumption by up to 15%. This high moment of inertia ensures the sheets do not sag even under heavy rain loads.

By choosing our optimized color coated roofing sheets, you balance cost and structural performance perfectly. Our solar-reflective coatings further reduce heat absorption by 30%, keeping internal environments cooler without expensive mechanical ventilation.

Architectural Versatility

Beyond strength, color coated roofing sheets offer immense architectural flexibility. Available in a wide palette, they allow architects to design industrial sheds that reflect corporate identity while maintaining a sleek, modern aesthetic.

The ability to curve and shape these color coated roofing sheets enables creative roof designs that are both functional and visually striking. The smooth, uniform coating ensures a premium appearance that lasts for years without compromise.

Environmental Sustainability

Modern color coated roofing sheets are 100% recyclable, making them an eco-friendly choice for sustainable construction. The steel can be repurposed at the end of its lifecycle, significantly reducing the carbon footprint of your industrial building.

Choosing color coated roofing sheets is a step towards greener development. Their reflective properties reduce the urban heat island effect, balancing industrial power with environmental responsibility for a cooler surrounding community.

Technical Excellence

Advanced Industrial Application & Quality Metrics

Structural Load Analysis

When designing large-span warehouses in Navi Mumbai, the structural performance of color coated roofing sheets is a critical variable. Our engineering team conducts rigorous load-testing simulations to ensure that the trapezoidal profile can withstand static loads from HVAC units and dynamic loads from maintenance personnel. By optimizing the crest height and valley width, these color coated roofing sheets provide superior drainage capacity, preventing water ponding during the heavy monsoon seasons of Maharashtra. This structural efficiency translates to longer purlin spans, which drastically reduces the amount of secondary steel required for the building's skeletal frame.

Coating Technology & Chemical Resistance

The longevity of color coated roofing sheets is determined by the multi-layered coating stack. Beyond the standard galvanizing, our sheets feature a specialized chemical pre-treatment layer that enhances paint adhesion. This is followed by a high-grade primer and a top-coat of Super Durable Polyester (SDP) or Silicon Modified Polyester (SMP). These advanced finishes ensure that the color coated roofing sheets remain resistant to industrial pollutants and coastal salinity. For chemical manufacturing plants in Taloja or Chakan, we offer customized coating thicknesses that provide an extra layer of protection against acidic vapors and corrosive fumes.

Installation Standards for Mumbai Industrial Zones

Proper installation is just as important as material quality. For color coated roofing sheets, we mandate the use of EPDM-washered fasteners that create a watertight seal at every penetration point. Our installation protocols include precise lap-joint sealing and ridge-cap detailing to eliminate any risk of seepage. Given the high wind speeds experienced in coastal Mumbai, we utilize specialized stitching screws and storm washers to secure the color coated roofing sheets against uplift forces. This meticulous approach ensures that your industrial shed remains zero-maintenance for decades, protecting your inventory and machinery from the elements.
