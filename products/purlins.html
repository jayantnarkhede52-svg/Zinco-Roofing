Technical Data Sheet

The Engineering Backbone of Modern PEBs

In Pre-Engineered Buildings (PEB), structural integrity heavily depends on the secondary framework. Zinco specializes in precision cold formed steel sections that offer superior strength-to-weight ratios over traditional hot-rolled iron.

Our C-Purlins are highly effective for simple spans. Meanwhile, our Z-Purlins feature nested ends for continuous-span roofing systems. This zinco purlins configuration drastically increases the load-bearing capacity for wide industrial structural members, making them essential for mega-warehouses across the greater Mumbai industrial corridor.

High Tensile Steel

Premium cold formed steel sections with yield strengths up to 550MPa allow for much longer spans without sacrificing stability.

Deep Galvanization

A heavy zinc coating of 120-275 GSM ensures 25+ years of intense rust protection. This justifies the galvanized purlin price instantly.

Rapid Assembly

Factory-punched alignment holes in zinco purlins guarantee a perfect fit every time, cutting down manual labor on site.

Technical Insights

Why High-Tensile Zinco Purlins Change Everything

Weight Reduction

Historically, warehouse roofs required massive hot-rolled materials. These were incredibly heavy and expensive to transport. By utilizing cold formed steel sections, we achieve an astronomically higher strength-to-weight ratio. This means you can build wider structures using fewer industrial structural members. This direct reduction in dead-weight provides massive cost savings, far offsetting the initial galvanized purlin price.

The Power of Overlapping

Our highly engineered zinco purlins feature asymmetrical flanges. This allows the end of one Z-purlin to nest perfectly inside another over a support column. This overlapping technique creates continuous industrial structural members across multiple bays. It literally doubles the structural rigidity at stress points, preventing roof sagging even under extreme wind uplift conditions or when supporting heavy solar panel loads.

Corrosion Resistance

Standard painted structural steel eventually succumbs to rust in humid environments. As an expert, we ensure our cold formed steel sections are heavily galvanized. Zinco purlins are coated in a thick layer of zinc during milling. This means that paying the premium galvanized purlin price guarantees your building's skeleton remains structurally sound and rust-free for decades.

Custom Fabrication for Layouts

Standardized components often fail in specialized architecture. We offer total customization of our cold formed steel sections. We roll-form zinco purlins to any required length (up to 12 meters) and provide custom hole-punching patterns to match your drawings.

This absolute precision means that whether you are building a solar frame or a heavy-duty storage warehouse, the zinco purlins fit instantly upon delivery, massively streamlining your construction timeline.

Structural Engineering FAQ

Is the galvanized purlin price worth the investment over painted steel?

Absolutely. While the initial galvanized purlin price is marginally higher, the total elimination of rust maintenance and painting makes zinco purlins significantly cheaper over a 20-year span.

Why choose Z-Purlins over standard C-Purlins?

Z-shaped cold formed steel sections are highly preferred for multi-bay buildings because their shape allows them to overlap at supports, creating continuous industrial structural members that increase load efficiency.

Can I get custom components for specific designs?

Yes, as a highly versatile C and Z Purlins Manufacturer in Navi Mumbai, we can custom-punch and cut any length of zinco purlins to match your exact structural blueprints.
