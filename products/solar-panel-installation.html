Industrial Client FAQ

Will the drill holes for mounting cause rust?

We use bi-metal screws with integrated EPDM washers and high-grade sealants. As the top specialist for **Solar Panel Installation in Navi Mumbai**, we guarantee the structural and weather-tight integrity of your roof.

Can I install solar on an old asbestos roof?

We generally advise against it due to the fragility of asbestos. However, we can perform a 'Roof Over-Sheeting' project first, providing a new metal surface ideal for solar mounting.
