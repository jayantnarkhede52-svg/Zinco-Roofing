Reimagining the Mangalore Classic

Traditional clay roofs look stunning, but they come with severe structural weight and a high risk of cracking. We designed our Metal Roofing Tile Sheets in Navi Mumbai to solve this exact problem. You get the gorgeous, wavy aesthetic of traditional Mangalore tiles, but backed by the unbreakable strength of pre-painted galvalume steel.

These sheets are precision-pressed to mimic natural ceramic tiles perfectly. Because they arrive in large, continuous panels rather than individual brittle blocks, they form an incredibly tough barrier. This makes them highly preferred for coastal bungalows in Alibaug, luxury resorts, and high-end industrial executive blocks.

Advanced Multi-Layer PVDF Coating

We understand that the number one fear with metal roofing is the paint fading or peeling away. To prevent this, we utilize a highly resilient PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) coating system. This multi-layered paint technology is baked directly into the steel at massive temperatures.

What does that mean for you? It means the rich terracotta, forest green, or charcoal grey colors will stay vibrant for well over a decade, resisting harsh UV rays without chalking. It is tailor-made to survive the salt-heavy coastal environments found in Maharashtra.

Deep Dive: Homeowner FAQ

Do metal tiles heat up the house intensely?

Not entirely. They have a high Solar Reflectance Index (SRI), meaning they reflect much of the sun's harsh heat before it enters. When paired with a small layer of bubble wrap insulation beneath, they actually keep your interiors significantly cooler than heat-absorbing concrete or thick clay.

Are they noisy during monsoon season?

The stepped profile actually breaks the acoustic vibration of rain drops much better than a flat sheet. If you install them over a solid wooden deck or with basic underlayment, the noise levels are virtually indistinguishable from ceramic tiles.
