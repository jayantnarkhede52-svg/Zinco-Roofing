Reimagining the Mangalore Classic

Zinco's Metal Roofing Tile Sheets in Navi Mumbai offer the timeless elegance of traditional Mangalore clay tiles without the structural baggage. Each sheet is precision-pressed from high-grade pre-painted galvalume steel, creating a stepped profile that provides superior water drainage while mimicking the rhythmic beauty of ceramic tiling.

Whether you are designing a coastal resort in Alibaug or an executive office block in a Navi Mumbai industrial park, our metal tiles provide the "premium" look that standard corrugated sheets simply cannot achieve. They are the ideal choice for projects where aesthetics are as critical as structural integrity.

Our Metal Roofing Tile Sheets in Navi Mumbai are equipped with an advanced PVDF coating system that resists UV radiation and chemical pollutants. This ensures that the vibrant terracotta, charcoal, or forest green colors do not fade, even under the harsh tropical sun of Maharashtra.

Deep Dive: Metal Tile FAQ

Do metal tiles heat up the house?

Actually, because they have a high solar reflectance index (SRI), they reflect a large portion of the sun's energy. When installed with a small air gap or insulation, they can keep interiors cooler than traditional clay tiles.

Are they suitable for the extreme Navi Mumbai monsoons?

Absolutely. The interlocking design and superior water-tightness make them far more reliable than individual clay tiles, which can displacement or crack during heavy storms.
