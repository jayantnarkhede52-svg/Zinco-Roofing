The Critical Sealing Interface

A roof is only as strong as its weakest joint. While primary sheets provide coverage, it is the accessories that ensure a building is truly weather-tight. As a dedicated **Roofing Accessories Manufacturer in Navi Mumbai**, Zinco produces a comprehensive range of ridges, flashings, and trims designed specifically for the aggressive coastal climate of the Konkan region.

Our ridge caps are cold-formed from the same high-tensile steel as our primary roofing sheets, ensuring a perfect color match and identical thermal expansion rates. This eliminates the "gapping" issues common when using third-party accessories, preventing water ingress at the roof's highest points.

High-Volume Gutters

Designed to handle 100mm/hour rainfall intensity, our gutters feature deep profiles and reinforced brackets for zero overflow.

Class 3 & 4 Fasteners

We supply only high-grade self-drilling screws with EPDM washers that resist UV degradation and chemical corrosion.

Custom Flashings

From gable trims to corner flashings, every component is custom-bent to your building's specific geometry.

Complete Hardware Integration

Beyond sheets and steel, a modern industrial facility requires integrated ventilation and lighting solutions. As the premier **Roofing Accessories Manufacturer in Navi Mumbai**, Zinco integrates turbo ventilators and polycarbonate skylights directly into the roofing schedule.

We provide FRP (Fiber Reinforced Plastic) base plates custom-molded to match your roof's rib profile, ensuring that any penetration of the roof surface remains 100% leak-proof for the life of the building.

Professional Hardware FAQ

Do you provide the screws needed for installation?

Yes, as a holistic **Roofing Accessories Manufacturer in Navi Mumbai**, we supply high-tensile self-drilling screws (SDS) with integrated washers to ensure every point of attachment is leak-proof and rust-resistant.

Can you match the color of accessories to my old roof?

While we carry a wide range of standard colors (RAL shades), we can also fabricate accessories from plain galvanized steel or aluminum for projects where a color match isn't required.
