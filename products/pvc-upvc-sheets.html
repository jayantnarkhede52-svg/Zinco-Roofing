The Science of Multi-Layer Protection

Being a leading **UPVC Roofing Sheets Manufacturer in Navi Mumbai** means going beyond the basics. Our sheets feature a core layer of thermal insulation sandwiched between two rigid, corrosion-resistant layers. This "sandwich" construction creates a roof that is not just a shield, but a smart thermal barrier.

This innovative design ensures that factories in high-temperature zones like Panvel experience significantly lower cooling costs, while the external ASA layer provides the "Weatherability" required to withstand decades of direct UV exposure without becoming brittle.
