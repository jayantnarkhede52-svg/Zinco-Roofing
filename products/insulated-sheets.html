Advanced Thermal Insulation Technology

When it comes to maintaining a stable internal environment, our Insulated Puff Panel Sheets in Navi Mumbai are the industry benchmark. These panels consist of a rigid core of CFC-free Polyurethane Foam (PUF) sandwiched between two high-quality color-coated steel sheets.

This triple-layer construction provides an exceptionally low thermal conductivity (K-value), effectively trapping cool air inside and reflecting external heat. For industrial facilities in Navi Mumbai's Taloja industrial belt, this translates to a 30-40% reduction in air conditioning costs and significantly higher protection for heat-sensitive inventory like pharmaceuticals and food products.

Energy Efficiency

Maximize thermal retention and minimize HVAC load with a continuous insulation barrier.

Hygienic & Anti-Fungal

Food-grade coatings make these panels ideal for cold storage and clean room applications.

High Mechanical Strength

The core-to-steel bond provides superior rigidity for long spans and high wind loads.

Engineered for Navi Mumbai Industrial Humidity

The Insulated Puff Panel Sheets in Navi Mumbai we manufacture are specifically coated to withstand the high humidity and salt-laden air of coastal Maharashtra. Our tongue-and-groove lacing system ensures a 100% airtight and waterproof joint, preventing thermal leakage and moisture ingress.

Whether you are building a new temperature-controlled warehouse or upgrading an existing PEB shed, our PUF panels offer the fastest installation turnaround in the market, allowing you to commence operations without delay while ensuring structural compliance with international standards.
