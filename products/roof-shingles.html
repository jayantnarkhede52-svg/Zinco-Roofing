Why Choose Zinco Shingles?

Unmatched Durability in Coastal Climates

Engineered Defense

The coastal climate in Navi Mumbai—characterized by high humidity and saline air—is brutal on traditional clay tiles and standard metal sheets. Zinco shingles are engineered with a non-woven fiberglass mat coated in weathering-grade asphalt. This creates an impenetrable barrier that resists rot, rust, and cracking.

UV & Algae Protection

The top layer features ceramic-coated mineral granules that reflect harmful UV rays. More importantly, our premium variants include copper-lined granules that actively prevent blue-green algae growth. This ensures your roof maintains its pristine, freshly-installed color for decades.

Cyclone-Grade Bonding

High-altitude winds require a roof that won't lift. Our shingles utilize thermal-activated adhesive strips. Once exposed to sunlight, these strips fuse the overlapping layers into a single monolithic membrane capable of withstanding extreme wind uplift forces up to 200 km/h.

Exceptional ROI

When comparing the overall asphalt roofing price against its 25-50 year lifespan, the value is undeniable. The combination of zero maintenance, immense aesthetic upgrade, and superior structural integrity means partnering with Zinco ensures a highly cost-efficient, lifelong roofing solution.

Homeowner FAQ

Are shingles too heavy for my existing roof structure?

Not usually. We always conduct a structural assessment. While shingles are heavier than basic metal sheets, they are easily supported by most standard wooden or steel trusses.

Do they fade under the harsh sun?

No. The ceramic-coated granules are highly UV-resistant, designed to maintain their vibrant color for over 25 years without fading, peeling, or cracking.
