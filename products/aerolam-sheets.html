The Science of Modern Radiant Barriers

Standard roofing sheets, whether metal or asbestos, act as massive heat radiators during the intense Navi Mumbai summer. Zinco's Aerolam Insulation Sheets in Navi Mumbai are designed to break this cycle. Unlike bulk insulation which only slows down heat transfer, Aerolam uses pure aluminum foil to reflect 97% of radiant heat back out of the building.

This reflective technology is coupled with a cross-linked polyethylene (XPE) or bubble core that provides a thermal break, preventing conductive heat from reaching the factory floor. The result is a facility that stays up to 10°C cooler, drastically reducing the load on HVAC systems and improving worker productivity.

Insulation Deep Dive

Can Aerolam be installed under an existing roof?

Yes, we specialize in "retro-fitting" Aerolam Insulation Sheets in Navi Mumbai. We use a wire-mesh or tensioning system to install the barrier under your existing metal or asbestos roof without needing to dismantle the structure.

How does Aerolam differ from PUF insulation?

PUF slows move heat through conduction. Aerolam stops the heat before it enters by reflection. While PUF is thicker and better for cold storage, Aerolam is more cost-effective and efficient for standard factory heat reduction.
